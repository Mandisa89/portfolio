Welcome

I am a creative and detail-oriented front-end developer based in Johannesburg, South Africa.

I have been building cost-effective, maintainable websites for clients from initial design to live site for the last 4 years.

I hold a BA Honours in Motion Picture Medium with a major in Cinematography, and spent my first professional decade as a freelance camera technician in the film and advertising industry. While programming was originally a part-time several-year-long exploration in how to help good businesses better reach their customer-base, I am now fully committed to this career and am excited to be using my skillset to build solutions to real-life problems within my community.
